The Ninja Way - a large-scale Naruto mod for Terraria

Features:
- Chakra system (regeneration 2/sec)
- 6 clans with unique bonuses (Uchiha, Hyuga, Uzumaki, Senju, Naka, Lee)
- 5 elements with passive effects and techniques
- Sharingan, Byakugan, Mangekyo, Rinnegan
- Techniques: Earth, Fire, Wind, Water, Lightning
- Level system (+10 max chakra per level)
- Lee's Gates (8 levels of power)
- Kage title at level 30

Controls:
Z - Earth Wall
X - Fireball
V - Wind Bullet
C - Water Bullets (3 shots)
F - Lightning (chain)
K - Sharingan
J - Byakugan
L - Clan Ability / Lee's Gates
M - Mangekyo
N - Susanoo
B - Kamui
Y/U/I - Rinnegan (with Mangekyo)
T/G - Kage abilities (after level 30)
H - Show chakra
